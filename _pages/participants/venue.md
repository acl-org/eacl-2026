## Conference Venue

**[Palais des Congrès Rabat Bouregreg](https://www.palais-bouregreg.ma/index.html)**  
The Rabat Bouregreg Convention Centre

Discover the Palais des Congrès Rabat Bouregreg – Morocco’s premier destination for world-class events.  
Nestled in the heart of the Bouregreg Valley between the historic medinas of Rabat and Salé, this modern convention center blends cutting-edge technology with timeless Moroccan elegance.

Spanning over **11,000 m²**, it offers versatile spaces for conferences, exhibitions, and celebrations, accommodating from **20 to 1,200 guests**.  
Just minutes from **Rabat–Salé Airport** and major transport hubs, the Palais des Congrès ensures convenience, comfort, and an unforgettable experience.

## Transportation

### Recommended Transportation Options

- **Trains (ONCF)** – Efficient connections to Casablanca, Kenitra, Tangier, Marrakech  
- **Trams (Rabat–Salé Tramway)** – Modern network across the city  
- **Petit Taxis** – Short trips within Rabat (blue taxis)  
- **Grand Taxis** – Intercity and shared routes  
- **Buses** – Inexpensive public transport  
- **Walking** – Pedestrian-friendly central areas  

---

### From Rabat–Salé Airport to the Venue

- **Grand Taxi / Private Transfer** – 15–20 minutes  
- **Car rental / ride-hailing** – Convenient alternative  

---

### Public Transportation Etiquette

- Allow passengers to exit before boarding  
- Offer seats to elderly and disabled passengers  
- Keep noise low  
- Validate tickets before boarding

## From Casablanca Mohammed V International Airport (CMN) to Rabat – Centre Ville

There are several ways to travel from Casablanca Airport (CMN) to Rabat city center:

### By Train (Most Convenient & Affordable)
* **From:** Aéroport Mohammed V Train Station (inside the airport – follow the signs).
* **To:** Rabat Ville Station or Rabat Agdal Station.
* **Duration:** around 1 hour 15 minutes.
* **Price:** approximately 60–100 MAD (6–10 USD).
* **Frequency:** every hour.

**Steps:**
1. After landing, follow the signs to the train station inside the airport.
2. Buy a ticket at the counter or on the machine.
3. Take the train direction Rabat or Kenitra.
4. Get off at Rabat Ville or Rabat Agdal (both are in the center).

### By Taxi (Fast but More Expensive)
* **Duration:** about 1 hour (depending on traffic).
* **Price:** between 700 and 900 MAD (70–90 USD).
* **Type:**
  * Grand Taxi (shared or private)
  * Private Airport Transfer (booked online)

## From Rabat–Salé Airport (RBA) to Rabat – Centre Ville

Rabat–Salé Airport is located about 10 km from the city center. Here are the main transportation options:

### Taxi (Most Convenient)
* **Duration:** 20–25 minutes
* **Price:** around 100–150 MAD (10–15 USD)
* **Type:** Grand Taxi (white color)
* **Where to find it?** Taxis are available directly outside the terminal.
    * Recommended if you have luggage or arrive at night.

### Shuttle Bus (Cheaper Option)
* **Line:** ALSA Bus Shuttle – Line 2
* **Destination:** Gare Rabat Ville (city center train station)
* **Duration:** approx. 30 minutes
* **Price:** 20 MAD
* **Frequency:** every 30 minutes to 1 hour
* **Service hours:** usually from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM

## From Casablanca Mohammed V International Airport (CMN) to Palais des Congrès Bouregreg

The most convenient and recommended option is the ONCF Train.

1. **At the Airport:** Follow signs to the "Aeroport Mohammed V" train station, located directly at the airport.
2. **Take the Train:** Board a train heading to Rabat-Ville (the main station in Rabat). Trains run frequently (approx. every hour). The journey takes about 1 hour and 45 minutes.
3. **Final Leg:** From Rabat-Ville Station, the Palais des Congrès is about 3 km away.
    * **By Taxi:** Take a petit taxi (small, beige-blue taxi). Ensure the meter is running or agree on a price first (approx. 15-20 MAD).
    * **By Tram:** Take the tram heading towards "Hay Karima" or "Université" and get off at the "Palais des Congrès" stop.
4. **Alternative:** Pre-book a private transfer or Grand Taxi for a door-to-door service.

## From Rabat–Salé Airport (RBA) to Palais des Congrès Bouregreg

Rabat-Salé Airport is much closer to the city center.

* **By Taxi (Most Direct):** Petit taxis are available. The journey takes 15-20 minutes.
* **By Bus:** The L22 Airport Shuttle Bus connects the airport to the city center, with stops near the train station. From there, take a taxi or tram to the Palais des Congrès.
